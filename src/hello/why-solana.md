# Why Solana

## Reasons

- A Smart Contract using `Rust`.
- [Fast](https://break.solana.com/)/Cheap/[Green](https://solana.com/environment).
- Use by `VISA`: https://www.helius.dev/blog/evaluating-solana-for-enterprise-use-a-comprehensive-guide
